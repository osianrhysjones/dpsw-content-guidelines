# CDPS Content Guidelines

Welcome to the content guidelines for the **Centre for Digital Public Services (CDPS)**.

These guidelines help content designers, translators and other team members create
consistent, clear and accessible content in English and Welsh.

## What's in here

- **Getting started** — an introduction to content principles at CDPS
- **Content process** — how to plan, write, and manage content through its lifecycle
- **Writing and design** — practical guidance on how we approach content design
- **Formatting and patterns** — rules for punctuation, dates, headings, links, and more
- **Content model** — the content types, components and taxonomy used on the CDPS website
- **Bilingual glossary** — agreed English/Welsh terms with usage notes
- **Community** — resources for translators and the Content Design Cymru community
