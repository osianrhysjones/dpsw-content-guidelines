# Contractions

> ✅ Use non-negative contractions – for example
> - they've
> - we'll

Avoid using ‘should've', 'could've', or 'would've' as these are hard to read.

Do not use negative contractions, for example, ‘don't’ and ‘can't’. 

Strong evidence shows users misread these and risk making the wrong decisions.

### Hyphens and dashes

Hyphenate:

- re + word starting with ‘e’, for example re–elect
- co-ordinate

Do not hyphenate:

- reuse
- reorder
- reopen
- email
- antisocial

Do not use a hyphen unless it’s confusing without it, for example, a little used-car is different from a little-used car.

Use ‘to’ for time and date ranges, not hyphens.
