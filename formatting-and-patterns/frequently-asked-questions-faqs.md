# Frequently asked questions (FAQs)

> We do not use frequently asked questions.

For more information, go to Tom Adam’s [Co-op Digital blog post, ‘Why FAQ’s aren’t the answer you’ve been looking for'](https://digitalblog.coop.co.uk/2018/09/13/why-faqs-arent-the-answer-youve-been-looking-for/).

Research shows that in the vast majority of situations they make it are harder for users to find what they’re looking for.
