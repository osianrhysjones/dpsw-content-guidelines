## Format

This must be published as HTML on digitalpubicservices.gov.wales

Talk to the communications team about how to plan, write, trsnslate and publish this content.

## Do not

Write end of project reports as information decks. This is not accessible and not great for presenting.

You may want to create accompanying presentation decks to share your work. Read about presentation decks on the Comms playbook.
