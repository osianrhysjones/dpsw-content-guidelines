# Instructions for users

Related content types: Service manual article (../../Content%20types/Service%20manual%20article%2010aca103050680a4a3cafbc451f2902e.md)

We need something about how to clearly instruct users when suggesting, instructing or demanding something. 

Consider our values and tone of voice. Do we need variations for different content types/communication methods?

### When to use

You can (or not)

You must…

You should…

Consider doing….

Make sure that…

All of these may be valid, but recognising when is key to help our consistency
