# People profile

Related content types: Profile (../../Content%20types/Profile%20024841af3a2a4d738c2d187b31256f6c.md)

People profiles are used for:

- Advisory panel members
- Chair and board members
- Members of SLT

Profiles do not have their pwn specific pages, they appear on a listing page for each cohort they belong to.

## Purpose of people profiles

The purpose of these pages is to show how we’re experts in our field, and use expertise to help our work.

## Contents

This content should include

- Full name
- Job title
- Portrait photo
- A short biography
- LinkedIn and Twitter profile links.

### Writing a biography

A biography should be slightly different, depending on which cohort an individual belongs to.

- Leadership team: A profile here should explain what this person’s role is in CDPS today, and how they’re making a difference.
- Advisory panel: This should be a brief explanation of the person’s expertise and professional interests. This should be relevant to advising CDPS.
- Chair and board members: This should be a brief explanation of the person’s expertise and professional interests. This should be relevant to steering CDPS.

### Don’t include

There are some things we don’t include on a CDPS biography:

- Career histories (a LinkedIn profile offers this)
- Unrelated personal hobbies (Twitter, and other social media, can cater for this)
