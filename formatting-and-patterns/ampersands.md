# Ampersands

Use ‘and’ rather than ‘&', unless it’s part of a:

- brand or product name
- company, organisation or department’s name
- logo

‘And’ is easier for people to skim and understand.

For more information see the [gov.uk blog post on ‘Ampersands, date ranges and contractions: style guidance’](https://insidegovuk.blog.gov.uk/2014/02/25/ampersands-date-ranges-and-contractions-style-guidance-2/).

> We don’t use ampersand in Cymraeg, ever.
