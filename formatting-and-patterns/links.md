# Links

> ⛔
> Do not use vague words such as ‘click here’ or ‘more information’ as link text.

Links should be used to:

- help users get to where they need to go
- avoid duplicating information that exists elsewhere
- link to an authority on a subject.

Descriptive link text helps:

- people who use screen reading software
- users to are likely to scan the page

> Bad example:
> [Click here](legal-content.md) to see information about bus passes

> Better examples:
> for free travel on buses in Wales [apply for a bus pass online](links.md)
> [Apply for free bus pass](links.md) to get free travel on buses in Wales
