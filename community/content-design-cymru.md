## Processing new members

### How people join

People sign up by [filling in the form on our website](https://digitalpublicservices.gov.wales/courses-and-events/communities-practice/content-design-cymru).

This triggers an email to the content.design@ mailbox.

We then need to:

1. make sure new members get a joining email [using the new joiners template](https://digitalpublicservices.sharepoint.com/:w:/r/sites/Connect/_layouts/15/Doc.aspx?sourcedoc=%7B981BCD44-DFF1-4766-A8F1-C71FD8ED3D4E%7D&file=Template%20-%20content%20design%20community.docx&action=default&mobileredirect=true)
2. add them to the calendar invite for the Main community meetups
3. add them to our Basecamp platform

### Enquiries

If people directly ask us to join the community, redirect them [fill in the form on our website](https://digitalpublicservices.gov.wales/courses-and-events/communities-practice/content-design-cymru). 

This makes sure we’ve got all the data and that they’re added to Hubspot.

> “Dear xxx,

Thank you for your interest in participating of our Community. 😊

[Register on our website](https://digitalpublicservices.gov.wales/courses-and-events/communities-practice/content-design-cymru) and tick the box to say you consent to receiving our emails, so that you can receive all our comms and invites.

We’re looking forward to seeing you in our community meetings!

Have a lovely day,
xxxx
>
